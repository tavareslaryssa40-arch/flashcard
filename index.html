Global Communication Flashcards

What is the “follow-the-sun” model in global teams?
It is a strategy where teams in different time zones work sequentially, allowing projects to progress almost 24 hours a day.
Why can silence in global meetings be misleading?
Because in some cultures silence signals respect or careful reflection, not disengagement or lack of ideas.
Why can language fluency distort perceptions of competence?
Because strong language skills may be mistaken for expertise, while valuable contributions from non-fluent speakers may be overlooked.
